Blade Runner (1982)

Los Angeles, 2019. Humanity has reached new heights of technological prowess, but in the process has caused irreversible damage to the environment and their own mental stability. Rick Deckard is a blade runner, a cop who specializes in hunting down and "retiring" replicants, genetically-engineered beings created to do the dangerous and degrading work that humans won't do. When four replicants escape from an offworld colony and make their way to Earth, Deckard is called in to track them down.

Deckard's investigation leads him to a rogue replicant named Rachael, who is different from the others because she doesn't know she's a replicant. As Deckard begins to fall in love with Rachael, he must also confront the morality of his job and the existential implications of replicants that are becoming more human than their creators.
